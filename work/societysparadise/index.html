About Society's Paradise

Society's Paradise is a luxury clothing brand based in London. Recently, they opened a fully functional virtual store.

What we did...

  • The first part of our job was to design an illustration for clothing, for both t-shirts and hoodies. For that we created a unique design. Each of them has the items positioned differently, so it looks better depending on the cothes you're wearing.

  • The second part of our work at Society's Paradise was branding. More concretely we designed a simple and minimal business card, only containing the essential information of the shop, so the client can easily interact with them.
