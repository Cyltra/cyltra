Deures 3r ESO

Deures 3r ESO is a catalan website that displays all the homework and exams the students of IES Balàfia have.

What we did...

  • For IES Balàfia, we had to do a website so all their students could see what homework they have and when they have exams. Aaaand that's what we did!

    First of all we created two columns, one with the homework and another one with the exams.

    Under that we have a small calendar that shows all the exams the students have.

    And lastly, we have the students' timetable.

    The website is fully responsive and also counts with a login system for the teachers so they can post the students' tasks.
